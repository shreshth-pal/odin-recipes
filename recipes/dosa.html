Dosa

The pefect meal for the tropical soul

A dosa is a thin pancake or crepe originating from South India.

The fermented batter predominantly consisting of lentils and rice. It is somewhat similar to a crepe in appearance

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup idli rice or parboiled rice or regular rice -100gm
  • 1/4 cup urad dal
  • 1/8 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 2 tablespoons thick poha
  • 1.5 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon rock salt
  • oil

Recipe

  1. Soak lentil, rice, urad dal and fenugreek seed in 1.5 cup water for 5-6 hours
  2. Grind the soaked ingredients and 3/4 water and 1/2 tsp rock salt
  3. Heat a cast iron when the pan becomses hot spread 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon oil over the pan
  4. Take a ladle full of the batter.Pour the dosa batter and gently spread the batter starting from the center and moving outwards
  5. Fold and serve sada dosa hot.
